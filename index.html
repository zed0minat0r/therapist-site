About the Practice

A team that
gets it

Amy Fantalis & Associates is a small, local psychotherapy practice in downtown Media, PA. Our team of experienced therapists shares strong ethical and clinical values — and a deep commitment to meeting every client where they are.

With backgrounds spanning outpatient counseling, community mental health, and education, we bring real-world clinical experience to every session. We work closely with psychiatrists, primary care doctors, school psychologists, and guidance counselors to ensure integrated, whole-person care.
