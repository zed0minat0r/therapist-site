About the Practice

One therapist.
Real experience.
Deep care.

Laura Spaulding, LPC

Licensed Professional Counselor — Psychotherapy and Clinical Supervision

I built my practice on a simple belief: that good therapy is not a transaction — it is a relationship. The people who walk through my door deserve a space that feels honest, unhurried, and genuinely safe.

I keep my practice small by design because caseload size affects care quality. I collaborate closely with your other providers — psychiatrists, pediatricians, school counselors — because the best outcomes happen when everyone is aligned.

My experience spans school settings and outpatient counseling. That background shapes how I think about the whole person — not just the presenting problem, but the systems and relationships around them.
